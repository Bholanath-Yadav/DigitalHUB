# 💬 Digital HUB Live Chat - User Guide

## Getting Started

The **Live Chat** widget (blue bubble at the bottom-right) connects you directly with our AI support assistant and team. It's available 24/7 to help you!

---

## How to Use Live Chat

### Opening Chat
1. Click the **LIVE CHAT** bubble at the bottom-right corner of the screen
2. The chat window will open with a greeting message
3. Your chat session is automatically saved

### Sending Messages
1. Type your question or message in the input box
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send button** (arrow icon)
3. You'll see instant AI-powered responses for common questions
4. Staff members can take over if you need human assistance

### Closing Chat
- Click the **X button** in the top-right corner of the chat window
- Your conversation history will be saved for future reference

## Troubleshooting

**Chat not loading?**
- Refresh your browser (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear cookies and cache
- Try a different browser

**Messages not sending?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments
- Contact: **WhatsApp +977 9826749317**

**Lost chat history?**
- Don't worry! Your chat is saved with your session ID
- Reopen chat and your conversation will appear
